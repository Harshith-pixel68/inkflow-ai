## Overview

InkFlow AI is an intelligent engineering whiteboard designed for students, educators, researchers, and professionals.

It combines an infinite digital canvas with AI-assisted note-taking, engineering drawing tools, geometry utilities, circuit design tools, and rich notebooks into a single application.

The goal is to replace multiple engineering and study applications with one modern, fast, and intelligent workspace.
